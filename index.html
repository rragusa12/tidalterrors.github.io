Key Features

Keep track of time spent working

When you work for a buisnss you often clock in and out on a machine that records your hourse, however the time you worked isn't always shared with you.

Our app works to fix that by allowing you to keep track of your time with the simple press of a button.

Once you begin your shift simply open the app and press "Start" and when your shift ends press "Stop".

The time you worked that day will be automatically recorded in your personal records page which you can access through the button at the bottom of your screen.

Manage your Personal Time

OpenTime Tracker is a user-friendly app designed for efficient personal time management.

It simplifies time recording with start and stop buttons, making it easy to track various activities.

The records tab allows users to review their time logs and identify potential time overallocation,

promoting better time management decisions for increased productivity and work-life balance.

Personal Goal Tracking

OpenTime Tracker aids in personal goal achievement by enabling users to set time-related goals, track their progress, and adjust their routines accordingly.

This data-driven approach fosters accountability and informed decision-making, transforming aspirations into actionable steps for personal growth and success.
